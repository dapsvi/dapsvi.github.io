What is a Cardputer?

The Cardputer is a compact, wallet‐sized computer designed for portability and efficiency. It comes in several versions, each improving on performance, battery life, and functionality. Ideal for on‐the-go tasks, the Cardputer brings modern computing into a small form factor.

Versions

  • Cardputer V1: Basic functionality with essential features.
  • Cardputer V2: Enhanced performance and additional connectivity options.
  • Cardputer V3: Latest design with improved battery life and expanded features.

What is an M5Stick?

The M5Stick is a series of compact, versatile microcontroller development kits. Featuring an integrated display, buttons, and sensors, it is perfect for rapid prototyping, IoT projects, and educational purposes. Its modern design—with rounded edges and an ergonomic layout—ensures both functionality and style.

Popular Versions

  • M5StickC: A compact model with a 1.14-inch display and essential features.
  • M5StickC Plus: An enhanced version offering extra features and improved performance.
